Background

In my current role, I implement the company's diversity and inclusion strategy for my 60,000 colleagues. Most of my day is spent on coaching and facilitation, external engagement, data analysis and Managing Cummins’ 100+ employee resource groups.

  • Previous Roles

    In my previous role, I spent three years managing $12 million of annual charitable spending through the Cummins Foundation Community Development Grant program. I managed grantmaking over 60 countries, including the regions of Europe, Latin America, Africa, China, and more. Successful management of this portfolio required executing process improvement projects, developing tools for employee engagement, and overseeing strategic relationships with key community partners. Before joining Cummins, I worked for the national nonprofit KaBOOM! and the Indiana University Alumni Association.

How I work

There isn't a perfect one-size-fits-all methodology for each project, program, or relationship. I work with a suite of tools to design solutions that meet my customer's needs including:

Data Analysis

Using information to manage conflicting priorities and allocate resources appropriately.

Six Sigma

Leading projects that prioritize outcomes bases on consensus, data, and tools that utilize multiple voices.

Human Centered Design

A holistic approach to designing that listens to real individuals and imagines every touch-point with a process or product as an opportunity to improve the user experience.

Project Management

Bringing structure to large scale projects. Designing processes to mitigate risks and improve the outcomes of complex goals.

Multi-Cultural Leadership

Working with colleagues from around the world requires patience, trust, and knowledge of respected practices in key markets. I'm comfortable with working patterns on each continent.

Long-Term Partnerships

Innovation comes from fresh ideas, which require diverse perspectives and experiences. Strong relationship management drives new ideas.

Capacity Building

Understanding the financial, human capital, and external influences impacting an organization's growth. Identify areas for improvement to move forward.

Multi-Modal Communications

We all learn in different ways. I use writing, coaching, presentations, facilitation, blogging, social media management, podcasting and more to tell stories and share messages.
